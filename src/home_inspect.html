HOME INSPECTION

Package 1 - Traditional Inspection

A standard inspection primarily involves a visual examination of the accessible features of the property. Destructive testing is not permitted. The inspection is limited to what can be visually observed. This service is suitable for residential, commercial, and mixed-use residential/commercial properties. Each inspection includes a detailed description of any defects identified, documented in an electronic report that features pictures and illustrations. Pre-purchase Inspection This inspection is conducted on behalf of the buyer, typically after an Offer to Purchase has been made, with the condition that a home inspection must be completed. Pre-listing Inspection This inspection is carried out for the homeowner to identify issues and suggest corrective measures before listing the home for sale. The inspection will cover the following areas of the property:

  • Exterior systems
  • Roof systems
  • Structural systems
  • Plumbing systems
  • Electrical systems
  • Heating systems
  • Fireplaces and solid fuel-burning appliances
  • Air conditioning systems
  • Interior systems
  • Insulation and vapor barriers
  • Mechanical and natural ventilation systems

Package 2 - Extended Inspection

The Extended Home Inspection includes the use of advanced infrared camera technology. This camera can detect temperature variations that the naked eye cannot see, revealing issues such as moisture problems, heat loss, cold zones, and potential electrical faults. Infrared technology helps us accurately locate areas of concern, including but not limited to:

  • Roof and ceiling leaks
  • Plumbing leaks
  • Water intrusion, including hidden moisture damage that could lead to mold
  • Electrical issues, such as overheated wiring or circuit breakers
  • Energy efficiency problems, like heat loss and inadequate insulation or ventilation

These temperature variations appear as cold and hot spots on our camera screens, exposing hidden problems that might otherwise go unnoticed during a standard visual inspection.


Package 3 - Extended Inspection +

Our Extended + Inspection, includes everything from the Traditional and Extended inspection packages, plus air sampling for mold spores. We collect one air sample from outside the home and another from inside (typically in a central area of the basement). These samples are then sent to a laboratory for analysis, where a report is generated that identifies the types of mold spores present, their concentration, and the comparison between the exterior and interior samples to determine if elevated mold levels are present in the home.
